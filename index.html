Reading all works by them


This is my literary corner — a quiet space where I honor the writers who move me.

I’ve created this site to keep track of the authors I love and to challenge myself to read through each of their works. Every ticked box is a step deeper into their world, and every unticked one is a promise to return. This is my way of reading with intention, curiosity, and love.
